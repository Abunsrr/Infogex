About Us

About InfoGex Agency And It's Innovative IT Solutions

InfoGex established in 2019, is a dynamic startup on a mission to breathe new life into struggling businesses. With personalized solutions and Software Technology, we pave the path to success for ventures seeking transformation and growth.
Infogex offers IT Solutions to ensure your company's technology infrastructure supports its operational needs and growth objectives. We provide expert guidance to make informed technology-related decisions.

InfoGex, a Patna-based is a versatile software company offering web and app development, alongside business strategy services. Infogex also excels in crafting custom mobile apps for both Android and iOS, prioritizing user experience.
Beyond development, the company assists clients in formulating business strategies that align with market dynamics.
